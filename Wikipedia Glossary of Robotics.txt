<a href=""https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero_Moment_Point"" class=""mw-redirect"" title=""Zero Moment Point"">Zero Moment Point</a>|. Zero Moment Point is a concept related with dynamics and control of legged locomotion, e.g., for humanoid robots. It specifies the point with respect to which dynamic reaction force at the contact of the foot with the ground does not produce any moment, i.e. the point where total inertia force equals 0 (zero).
